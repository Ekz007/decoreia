Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

DecoreIA ("we", "us", "our") is operated by EKZ Apps. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using DecoreIA you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your name and email address (via email sign-up or Apple/Google Sign-In) and a unique user ID.
  • Content you provide — photos of rooms and objects you upload, used to generate your designs, videos and 3D results.
  • Purchases — records of subscriptions and in-app purchases (processed by Apple/RevenueCat; we do not receive your card details).
  • Usage data — which features you use, to improve the app (analytics).

How we use your data

  • To provide the app and generate your results.
  • To manage your account, wallet (diamonds) and purchases.
  • To improve and secure the service.

Third-party services

We share the minimum necessary data with trusted providers to run the app:

  • Supabase — authentication, database and storage.
  • Replicate — AI models that process your photos to generate results.
  • RevenueCat & Apple — purchases and subscriptions.
  • Apple & Google — sign-in.

We do not sell your data and we do not use it to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Data retention

Free-plan media is automatically deleted after 12 hours. Paid-plan media is stored while your account is active. You can delete your content or account at any time.

Your rights

You may access, correct or delete your personal data. To request deletion of your account and data, email us at suporte@decoreia.com.

Children

DecoreIA is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, access controls) to protect your data.
